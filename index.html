Operating Room Analytics

The concept for Syús evolved out of the quest for efficiency within the Anesthesia Medical Group (AMG). David Whitten, CEO, believed that staffing decisions for the OR ought to be driven by empirical data, which was not the case with block scheduling at most hospitals. He appointed David Reynolds to identify ways to more efficiently deploy the doctors and nurses of AMG. Reynolds and his task force rose to the challenge, developing an analytical framework that is the foundation of the present Syús solution. With the help of these unique, targeted analytics, AMG was able to handle more cases in less time, ultimately increasing both earnings for the company and staff satisfaction. Subsequently, Reynolds and his team successfully tested the offering at a sampling of hospitals, medical centers and anesthesia practices across the United States, affirming that the solution's benefits are replicable. In 2006, The Syús team began planning for a broad offering that was scalable to surgical facilities and anesthesia practices nationwide. A strategic partnership was formed with Airlift Group, LLC, which crafted a comprehensive suite of applications, including, for example, a scheduling functionality for doctors and nurses in the perioperative environment.

People today are overwhelmed, frustrated and confused by the sea of medical paperwork created by the healthcare system. At the same time, employers are struggling to avoid decreasing benefits or increasing premiums in an attempt to gain some control over the company's healthcare costs. And for both groups, reining in healthcare costs is difficult if those making the decision 'healthcare consumers' are completely disconnected from the impact of their healthcare choices and isolated from each other's experiences. Change Healthcare provides consumers the tools, services and information they need to make the best healthcare decisions possible.
